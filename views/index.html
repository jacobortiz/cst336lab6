Earth

Earth, our home, is the third planet from the sun. It's the only planet known
to have an atmosphere containing free oxygen, oceans of water on its surface and,
of course, life. Earth has a diameter of roughly 8,000 miles (13,000 kilometers)
and is round because gravity pulls matter into a ball. But, it's not perfectly round.
Earth is really an "oblate spheroid," because its spin causes it to be squashed at its
poles and swollen at the equator.

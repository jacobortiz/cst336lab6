Welcome to the Mercury page!

Mercury is the planet that is the closest to the sun. It is also the second smallest planet
in the solar system. Mercury rotates in a way that is unique in the Solar System. It is tidally
locked with the Sun meaning that relative to the fixed stars, it rotates on its axis exactly three
times for every two revolutions it makes around the Sun.

Welcome to the Venus page!

Venus is the second planet closest to the sun, is named from the Roman goddess of love and beauty.
Venus is the hottest world in the solar system. Although Venus is not the planet closest to the sun,
its dense atmosphere traps heat in a runaway version of the greenhouse effect that warms Earth.
As a result, temperatures on Venus reach 870 degrees Fahrenheit (465 degrees Celsius), more than
hot enough to melt lead.Probes that scientists have landed there have survived only a few hours
before being destroyed.
